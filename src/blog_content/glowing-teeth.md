# A Complete Guide to Healthy, Glowing Skin

Beautiful skin starts with a consistent skincare routine and healthy lifestyle habits. Here's everything you need to know to achieve and maintain glowing skin.

## 1. Cleanse Your Skin
Start and end your day with a gentle cleanser suitable for your skin type. Avoid over-cleansing, as it can strip your skin of natural oils.

## 2. Moisturize Daily
Moisturizing helps keep your skin hydrated and healthy. Choose a moisturizer that matches your skin type—whether it's dry, oily, or combination.

## 3. Protect from the Sun
Always wear sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher, even on cloudy days. UV rays can cause premature aging, sunburn, and skin cancer, so sun protection is essential.

## 4. Eat a Healthy Diet
Your skin reflects what you eat. Include foods rich in vitamins, antioxidants, and healthy fats, such as fruits, vegetables, nuts, and fish.

## 5. Get Enough Sleep
Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night. Sleep allows your skin to repair and regenerate, keeping it looking fresh and healthy.

Consistency is key to healthy skin—stick to your routine and be patient for results!
